Welcome to Pokébinderz!

Use this site to collect master sets of the Pokemon TCG in binders

1. One you got your account, navigate to the "Add Binder" page to start adding to your collection

2. On the "Collection" page, view the binders you've created by clicking into the pokeballs

3. On the page of the selected binder, search a set and map it to the binder

4. Now that you have a set mapped, add cards as you collect. You'll get you an alert for the card position once added.

Enjoy your collecting journey!
